What is 283 Production?

283 Production (or "283 Pro") is a fictional talent agency in The Idolmaster Shiny Colors, part of The Idolmaster franchise by Bandai Namco. It debuted in 2018 and focuses on producing idol groups for competitive performances and events. The agency is central to Shiny Colors, a mobile game where players manage and develop the careers of young idols, guiding them through training, personal growth, and interactions with fans. The storyline introduces the "Producer," a pivotal figure who mentors these idols and forms them into teams based on their personalities and talents, including units like illumination STARS, L'Antica, Houkago Climax Girls and more..

Each unit in 283 Production has a unique appeal and musical style, which appeals to various fans within the game's community. The idols in Shiny Colors are also spotlighted in adaptations like anime and manga, which add depth to their backgrounds and aspirations. This expansive content makes 283 Production a key setting that showcases the development of idols as they work toward becoming stars in the competitive world of The Idolmaster series.

283 is pronounced "Tsubasa", a wordplay on the Japanese word for "wings".
